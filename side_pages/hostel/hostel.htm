Hostel

Separate hostel accommodation is provided for boys and girls. There are four hostels for boys and one hostel for girls. All the hostels have common rooms, 24 hour Wifi-connectivity and recreational centres where newspapers, magazines and TVs are available. Round the clock water and electricity is provided. Laundry equipments are also available in the hostels.
